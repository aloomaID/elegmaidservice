East Los Angeles (Spanish: Este de Los Ángeles), or East L.A., frequently recognized by locals as 'East Los,' this region is an unincorporated area situated within Los Angeles County, California, United States. According to the United States Census Bureau, East Los Angeles is designated as a census-designated place (CDP) for statistical purposes.
